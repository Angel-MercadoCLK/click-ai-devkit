description: Turn an approved idea into an English PRD with scope, requirements, and acceptance criteria for Click Seguros.
---

## Workflow

1. Restate the problem and desired outcome.
2. Define in-scope and out-of-scope behavior.
3. Write functional requirements.
4. Add acceptance criteria that can be verified later.
5. Highlight unresolved product questions.

## Rules

- Write in English.
- Do not invent business policy.
- Keep the PRD useful for design, not bloated.
