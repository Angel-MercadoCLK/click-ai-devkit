# Verify

Read the spec, design, tasks, and apply record. Re-check the implementation directly. Run focused
tests and the relevant broader checks. Report blockers separately from warnings and suggestions in
English. Do not approve scope drift or unresolved critical failures. Continue to
`click-sdd-archive` only after a pass or pass-with-warnings result.
