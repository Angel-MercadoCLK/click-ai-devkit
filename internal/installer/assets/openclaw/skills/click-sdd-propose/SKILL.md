# Propose

Read the exploration notes when available. Write an English proposal containing the problem,
desired outcome, in-scope behavior, explicit non-goals, functional requirements, high-level
acceptance goals, and unresolved questions. Do not invent business policy or implementation details.
Continue to `click-sdd-spec` and `click-sdd-design` once the scope is clear.
