# Onboard

Explain the chain `explore -> propose -> spec/design -> tasks -> apply -> verify -> archive`.
Walk through one concrete example from the current repository. For each phase, explain what it
reads, what it writes, and why the order matters. Ask the developer what they want to inspect next;
do not silently perform a real change. Keep the walkthrough practical and in English for technical
artifacts.
